# 📖 Overview

**DUET RoadVision** is our solution for the **DUET CSE Carnival 2026 AI Hackathon**, where participants were challenged to build an AI model capable of detecting **13 different vehicle classes** from highway CCTV images.

With almost no prior practical experience in Computer Vision, our team **ShunnoThekeAI** developed, trained, and optimized the complete solution within just **48 hours**, competing against **140+ teams** from **42 universities** across Bangladesh.

Despite starting near the bottom of the leaderboard, we continuously improved our models and finished among the **Top 20 on the Public Leaderboard**.

# 🎯 Competition Challenge

Participants were required to:

- 🚗 Detect **13 different vehicle classes**
- 📷 Train using only **~810 labeled highway CCTV images**
- ⏱ Complete the entire project within **48 hours**
- 📊 Maximize the competition evaluation score
